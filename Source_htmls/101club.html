RIP John K. Kallman, 1951 - 2023
John was one of the most active ChipGuide Administrators, where his responsibilities included: New Zealand, Australia, North Dakota Charity Casinos, Minnesota, Military, Asian Poker Rooms and Nevada Route Tokens. He processed tens of thousands of user submissions into ChipGuide. As a collector, John contributed images of over 10,000 items from his collection to the ChipGuide. For his service to ChipGuide, he was awarded the Museum of Gaming History's Chairman's Award in 2022. John was a long-time Life Member of the CCA (LM 0112-112), a Convention Dealer and he was the CCA's Librarian from 2007 to 2016. John was an all-around great guy and a friend to everyone he met. He will be sorely missed.
